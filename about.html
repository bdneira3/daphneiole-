About Daphneiole

Daphneiole is a unique and interesting clothing brand based in Bandung since 2023. Not only does we represent a more refined and forward-thinking brand, we reflect that in each of our publications. Driven by a dreamy quest for power, living indifferent to our existence. Daphneiole is a dangerous freedom that seems lost over the years to describe the world objectively. Our first love has always been and will always be freedom, we try to give back to our roots through the products and opportunities we provide through the company, maintaining the respect of society. We continue to support our friends, who helped us drive and inspire our initial vision. Our goal for daphneiole was to evoke emotion then create a topic of discussion through our designs and boldly express our ideas in the hands of youth culture.
